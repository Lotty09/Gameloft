WHO WE ARE

Gameloft specializes in the development and publishing of video games that are predominantly geared toward mobile platforms, although they also extend their reach to consoles, PCs, and smart TVs. The company is renowned for its ability to create games that push the limits of mobile technology, offering console-quality experiences that can be accessed on smartphones and tablets. With a robust portfolio of games that span multiple genres, from action and racing to strategy and casual gaming, Gameloft’s titles are played by millions of gamers around the globe. Some of the company’s most successful franchises include Asphalt, Modern Combat, and Dungeon Hunter, all of which have become household names among mobile gamers. With over 20 studios across the world, Gameloft has a global presence, allowing them to tap into diverse markets and cultures, ensuring their games are both universally accessible and locally appealing.

OUR VISION

Gameloft’s vision is rooted in delivering fun and immersive gaming experiences to players worldwide. The company strives to stay at the forefront of mobile gaming innovation by leveraging new technologies, such as augmented reality (AR) and artificial intelligence (AI), to create more engaging and interactive games. Gameloft envisions a future where mobile games can rival the depth and complexity of console titles, offering rich storylines, multiplayer interactions, and cutting-edge graphics—all from the palm of your hand. They also place a strong emphasis on creating a community around their games. With features like competitive multiplayer modes, social integration, and live in-game events, Gameloft aims to foster connections between players and ensure that their games remain dynamic and engaging long after the initial download.

OUR MISSION

Gameloft’s mission is to bring quality gaming experiences to people of all ages, wherever they are. Whether on a phone, tablet, or smart TV, Gameloft strives to make their games accessible to as many people as possible by offering free-to-play titles alongside premium options. Their focus is on delivering innovative, entertaining, and engaging content, and they are committed to pushing the boundaries of mobile gaming technology. Additionally, Gameloft is dedicated to offering games that are inclusive and diverse. They aim to appeal to a broad demographic, ensuring that their titles resonate with different age groups, cultures, and gaming preferences. In an industry that’s constantly evolving, Gameloft’s mission is to stay ahead of the curve by embracing innovation while keeping the player experience at the center of their work.
